So, I took the risk of dumping my savings into a 6-month "coding bootcamp" to learn what I needed to start building a career in tech (while continuing working full-time).

By the end of the bootcamp, my greatest assets--my ability to solve problems and my curiosity to learn--allowed me to transition into my current role as a software engineer. More importantly, I found a home in software development.
